Join our team of instructors!

Potomac Code Camp is looking for bright young students to join our team this summer. If you are passionate about computer programming education and want to empower the next generation of makers, then this opportunity is for you!

About you

  • You are currently studying CS or have equivalent programming experience
  • You have prior experience tutoring or teaching and are an effective communicator
  • You are organized, timely, and work well in small groups
  • You are friendly, enthusiastic, comfortable and patient with children
  • You are as excited about code as we are!

About the position

Our summer schedule is broken up into blocks of classes, each two weeks long. For new instructors teaching their first class, the expected time commitment is roughly 70 hours, which includes 10 hours of training. For returning instructors, the expected time commitment is 50 hours.

Compensation for instructors is $900-$1300 per session, depending on enrollment.

Instructors are required to arrive half an hour prior to the beginning of class for setup and stay one hour after class has ended for wrap-up.

In addition, instructors will spend thirty minutes from home reviewing course material for the next day as well as updating campers' parents with their progress. For first time instructors, this may take longer.

How to apply

Please email teach@potomaccodecamp.com with the following:

  • A paragraph stating why you would like to join our team
  • Your summer availability
  • An attached resume
  • Any additional material you feel is relevant
  • Bonus - include a link to something (anything) you built with Scratch
